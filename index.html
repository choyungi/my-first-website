POST PRODUCTION

Post-production is part of the process of filmmaking, video production, audio production, and photography. Post-production includes all stages of production occurring after shooting or recording individual program segments.[1] Traditional (analog) post-production has mostly been replaced by video editing software that operates on a non-linear editing (NLE) system.
